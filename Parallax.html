Parallax Demo

Parallax scrolling is a web site trend where the background content is moved at a different speed than the foreground content while scrolling. Parallax is a displacement or difference in the apparent position of an object viewed along two different lines of sight, and is measured by the angle or semi-angle of inclination between those two lines. Due to foreshortening, nearby objects have a larger parallax than more distant objects when observed from different positions, so parallax can be used to determine distances.
